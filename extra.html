About Us


ELITE Tuition is the Qatar most successful mathematics tuition company offering complete range of A-Level and GCSE Mathematics programmes and tuition to students.


Tutopiya story began in 2018 when our CEO/Founder Nuha Ghouse was teaching students from many of the leading international schools in Singapore. Prior to moving to Singapore, she taught students from leading private schools in the UK. Nuha was determined to provide an effective live online tutoring experience as she wished to help her students who travelled long distances in public transport to her home. She realized the options for teaching live online were limited and ineffective and therefore embarked on developing her own live online tuition solution.
