Genius Kids

Genius Kids story began in 2018 when our CEO/Founder Mohamed Abdillahi was teaching students from many of the leading international schools in Qatar. Prior to moving to Qatar, he taught students from leading private schools in the UK.

Genius Kids

Mohamed was determined to provide an effective live online tutoring experience as he wished to help him students who travelled long distances in public transport to her home. He realized the options for teaching live online were limited and ineffective and therefore embarked on developing his own live online tuition solution.
